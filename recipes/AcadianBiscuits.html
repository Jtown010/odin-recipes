Acadian Biscuits

Description

Acadian biscuits are a traditional favorite. Sturdy and hearty, they are best served with molasses.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups white flour
  • 3 tbs baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg

Method

  1. In a large bowl combine the dry ingredients
  2. Cut in the butter until it resembles coarse crumbs and set aside
  3. Mix the milk with the egg then add to the dry ingredients
  4. Stir several times but do not overmix
  5. Turn out onto a flat surface and with a rolling pin flatten to 1/2 inch
  6. Cut out your biscuits with an empty tin can or cookie cutter and place on a greased baking sheet
  7. Bake at 350 degrees C for 12 minutes or until golden brown
