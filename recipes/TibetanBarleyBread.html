Tibetan Barley Bread

Description

A hearty dense loaf popular in the high Himalayas. One bite into this and you will be ready to summit Everest or philosophize with the Dali Lama. Also works well as a weapon to fight off amourous Yak.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups barley flour
  • 4 cups wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup millet meal (or roasted sunflower seeds or roasted sesame seeds)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons sesame or corn oil
  • 3 1/4 cups boiling water

Method

  1. Pan roast barley flour in the oil until darkened
  2. Mix flours together with salt
  3. Add oil, rubbing flour between hands until oily
  4. Add boiling water, using spoon to mix until dough begins to form
  5. Mix with hands, keep your hands cool by dipping them in a bowl of cold water
  6. Mix until it is the consistancy of a large earlobe
  7. Knead until smooth
  8. Place in an oiled pan and cut the top several times lengthwise
  9. Proof for 2-6 hours or overnight
  10. Bake at 450 degrees for 20 minutes on the middle rack then 400 for 40 minutes on the top rack
  11. Crust will be tough but inside tender
