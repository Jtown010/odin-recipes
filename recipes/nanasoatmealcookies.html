Nana's Oatmeal Cookies

Description

This recipe was a favorite of my Nana's. Whenever she knew I was visting she would have a batch cooling and ready for me to devour! With a rich chewy texture they are perfect comfort food for those chilly Nova Scotia nights.

Ingedients

  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup wheat germ
  • 1 cup margarine
  • 1 1/4 cups brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup choclate chips

Preheat your oven to 350 degrees C

In a large bowl combine flour and baking soda. Add the rolled oats, wheat germ and mix well.

In a seperate bowl, use an electric beater to cream the margarine, eggs, sugar and vanilla. Beat until nice and smooth, then add to the dry ingredients. Fold everything together well and then add the chocolate chips!

Form the cookies into balls and spread evenly on a greased baking sheet. Bake for 8-12 minutes until golden brown. Let them cool and enjoy!
