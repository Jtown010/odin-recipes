Nana's Oatmeal Cookies

Description

This recipe was a favorite of my Nana's. Whenever she knew I was visting she would have a batch cooling and ready for me to devour! With a rich chewy texture they are perfect comfort food for those chilly Nova Scotia nights.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup wheat germ
  • 1 cup margarine
  • 1 1/4 cups brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup choclate chips

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees C
  2. In a large bowl combine the flour, baking soda, rolled oats and wheat germ
  3. In a seperate bowl add margarine, eggs, sugar and vanilla and mix until smooth
  4. Add the margarine mixture to the dry ingredients and fold together
  5. Add the chocolate chips and mix again
  6. Form the cookies into balls and spread evenly on a greased baking sheet
  7. Bake for 8-12 minutes until golden brown. Let them cool and enjoy
